### 2. ğŸ§  Cause & Effect Brain (Causal Memory)

**Like when you:**
- Remember: "When I study the night before, I get better grades"
- Learn: "When I water plants, they grow better"

**How it works:** Every time the robot does something, it remembers what happened next. Then it can predict what will happen in the future!

**Real example:**

### 3. ğŸ¯ Trick Collection (Skill Library)

**What it does:** Every time the robot learns a new trick that works well, it saves it in its "trick collection" to use again!

**Like when you:**
- Learn to tie your shoes and can do it every time now
- Master a video game level and can beat it easily
- Remember the best way to organize your backpack

**How it works:** When the robot does something successfully many times (like 90 out of 100 tries!), it saves that trick as a "skill" and can use it whenever needed!

**Real example:**
- Homework Robot has a skill called "break_down_big_problems" - it learned that breaking big math problems into smaller steps works 94% of the time!

### 4. ğŸ¤” Learning from Mistakes (Reflexion)

- Burn cookies and set a timer next time

**How it works:** After every task, the robot asks itself "Could I have done that better?" and makes a note for next time!

**Real example:**
- Homework Robot noticed: "Last time I recommended really hard books, the student got frustrated. Next time I'll suggest easier books first, then harder ones!"
